About The Competition

CONNECT – State Bridge Design Competition is organized by the State Ministry of Rural Roads and other Infrastructure as a key milestone in the Rural Bridge Program which targets to develop 2000 rural bridges in 2021.

The State Ministry has identified following challenges in developing rural bridges of Sri Lanka.
  • Ever-increasing traffic and loads
  • Mobilization of construction material and equipment
  • The site and access constraints for construction
  • Schedule
  • Cost
  • Durability/Service life/Sustainability

Innovative engineered solutions may serve the program in overcoming all the challenges.

Duration

From 12th of January to 26th of March of 2021

Competition Phases

Letter of interest

A single page letter with the name of the institute, member(s) of the group, contact person, contact information, and a brief description of the idea (with a couple of sketches).

  • Phase One: Idea Submission

    Idea submission happens in two parts. Each idea has to be initially submitted as a single page proposal when registering, and then as the full idea submission as the second part.

  • Phase Two: Idea Presentation

    Selected idea submissions will be called up for presenting in front of a panel of experts.

Competition Categories

CONNECT State Bridge Design Competition is conducted under two categories.

School Category

For students

Students can enter as teams or as individuals with the approval of the Principal of the school.

Minimum Requirement:

A conceptual design of the bridge idea with a design brief highlighting the innovation.

University Category

For undergraduates

Undergraduates of any descipline can form teams with upto seven members and compete.

Minimum Requirement:

Computer aided model and comprehensive report with technical details. Competitors are encouraged to include any further items and be innovative.

Open Category

For professionals

Any individual or any other entity/organization can compete with a team of upto seven members.

Minimum Requirement:

Computer aided model and comprehensive report with technical details. Competitors are encouraged to include any further items and be innovative.
