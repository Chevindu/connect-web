About The Competition

About The Competition

CONNECT is a state bridge design competition, organized by the State Ministry of Rural Roads and other Infrastructure. This is a key milestone in Rural Bridge Program.

The State Ministry has identified following challenges in developing rural bridges in Sri Lanka.

  • Ever increasing requisite of capacity
  • Mobilization of construction material
  • Time, money and quality precincts

Innovative engineered solutions may serve the program in overcoming all the challenges.

Duration

From 12th of January to 26th of March of 2021

Competition Scope

Goal of CONNECT

CONNECT State Bridge Design Competition requires competitors to submit bridge design ideas to successfully overcome one or all of the challenges mentioned previously in rural bridges.

Competition Phases

  1. Idea Submission

    Idea submission happens in two parts. Each idea has to be initially submitted as a single page proposal when registering, and then as the full idea submission as the second part.

  2. Idea Presentation

    Selected idea submissions will be called up for presenting in front of a panel of experts.

Competition Categories

CONNECT State Bridge Design Competition is conducted under two categories.

University Category

For students

Undergraduates of any descipline can form teams with upto seven members and compete.

Minimum Requirement:

Computer aided model and comprehensive report with technical details. Competitors are encouraged to include any further items and be innovative.

Open Category

For professionals

Any individual or any other entity/organization can compete with a team of upto seven members.

Minimum Requirement:

Computer aided model and comprehensive report with technical details. Competitors are encouraged to include any further items and be innovative.

Winners' Prizes

1,000,000 LKR

500,000 LKR

250,000 LKR

Furthermore, exceptional ideas will be carried forward with the support of State Ministry in idealizing. Also, opportunities will be granted to exceptional idea holders to present at international research conferences and journals.

Competition Event Flow

  • 1. Appointment of expert panel
  • 2. Review of competition guidelines
  • 3. Competition launch and registration opening
  • 4. 1st info session (public)
  • 5. Registration closing
  • 6. Review of idea submissions by expert panel
  • 7. 2nd info session (for shortlisted teams)
  • 8. Idea presentations
  • 9. Review of idea presentations
  • 10. Awards night
